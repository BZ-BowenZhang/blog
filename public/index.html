About Me

I am Bowen Zhang, PhD student at the Department of Geography, King’s College London. I am currently conducting the PhD research project about Modelling Urban Clusters in Super Mega-city Region based on Human Mobility Pattern: A Case study of The Great Bay Area, China. My research interests mainly include: Utilising urban big data for analysing & identifying the human mobility pattern; Developing a quantitive framework to assess the urban spatial structure, especially in Mega-city regions; Spatial Interaction Models and their related applications
